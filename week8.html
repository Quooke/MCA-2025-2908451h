time-frequency analysis vs waveform-based analysis

The strength-time-frequency analysis is that it shows temporal and frequency information. Waveforms tend not to capture non-stationary dynamics. It shows how the frequency and amplitude change over time, showing the transition between verses, as you can see the sections differentiated by the gaps between the dense wavelengths.
